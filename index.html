About Me

Dian Damudi

Pixel Artist | Illustration Enthusias

I am Dian Damudi, an amateur Pixel artist and Illustration enthusias. I am living in Sukabumi, Indonesia with six cat who likes to colonize me :( .

Now i'm running an NFT project on Pentas.io, namely as an artist for the Tanah.io project. In addition, I am the Founder of Cici's Adventure on Pentas.io.

On below are some works for several projects and also exercises to practice my art skills. For me drawing something is a fun way to heal.
